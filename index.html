About Us

Epic Delivery is a reputable world wide-based branded company specializing in handling and shipping fine art, antiques, luxury items, and collectibles of all kinds through air, land and sea transportation across the whole world. We have been in this business since 1993, providing a full range of shipping services to private collectors, dealers, galleries, reputable business brands and auction houses.
